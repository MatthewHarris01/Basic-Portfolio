About Me

Except for a brief (3 year) stint as a performing and studio musician, I have spent my life designing and developing databases and business applications, writing how-to books, consulting, teaching, and training.

I've had the good fortune to work in a variety of industries with a variety of companies and institutions – large & small, public & private, non-profit and academic institutions. I’ve gained a large base of knowledge as a consequence of this wide experience, which enables me to successfully serve many types of client.

I take tremendous satisfaction from knowing that the applications I develop make people's lives easier and meet their unique needs. I have designed and built applications ranging from relatively simple estimators/calculators to complete systems that handle core tasks: customer service, contacts/sales, inventory, process tracking, project tracking, scheduling, donor management, clinical drug trials, and many more. I have written applications for commercial distribution as well as for my clients' in-house use.

I strive to create reliable, easily maintained and well-documented applications – it's a good feeling to deliver a reliable tool to the people who need it.

I am also a skilled technical writer. I am sole author, co-author, or a contributing author of over 20 technical how-to books, most of which teach database programming in Visual Basic and MS Access VBA, and VBA (Visual Basic for Applications) programming in MS Excel.
